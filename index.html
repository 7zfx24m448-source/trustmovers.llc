Additional Charges That May Apply

🏢 Stairs Surcharge

1st & 2nd floor are FREE. From 3rd floor and above: $50 per floor. Elevator is always free.

🚛 Travel Time

Always a flat 1-hour fee at your hourly rate. Covers movers driving from lot → your location → back to lot.

📦 Packing Materials

Boxes, packing paper, bubble wrap billed separately if requested. Partial or full packing service available.

🎹 Heavy & Oversized Items

Items over 150 lbs may incur extra charges. Piano, pool table, safe — ask us for a custom quote.

🌙 Overnight Storage

If needed between move days: 16ft truck $150/night, 26ft truck $300/night.

💳 Credit Card Fee

4% processing fee for credit/debit card. Zelle accepted with zero fees — preferred payment method.
